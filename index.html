Catagories

Crops

Crops are living plants grown by farmers. Most crops are foods such as grain, vegetables, or fruit. Crops are domesticated plants, most of which have been selected to improve their size, taste and other qualities. Many crops are farmed in monoculture.

Fruits

Fruits are important sources of vitamins and carbohydrates like fiber and sugar. They are low in calories and naturally sweet. Fruits and their juices are good sources of water, too. Different fruits contain different vitamins, so it is important to eat a variety of fruits.

Equipments

Agricultural machinery relates to the mechanical structures, from hand tools and power tools to tractors and the countless kinds of farm implements that they tow or operate. Diverse arrays of equipment are used in both organic and nonorganic farming. Agricultural machinery is an indispensable part of how the world is fed.
